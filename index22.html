About Me

Welcome to my portfolio. My name is Juan DeBiedma. I'm a graduate in chemical engineering from the University of Florida who wanted to dive deeper into web development. I'm also a professional gamer on Team Liquid under the alias Hungrybox. I was chosen as one of Forbes' 30 Under 30 in the Gaming category this year, and recieved the award for the 2016 ESPN Esports Moment of the Year.

While pursuing my degree I traveled around the country competing at major Super Smash Bros. Melee events to pay for my college education. While in college I also was a member of multiple acapella groups, a table tennis league, professional organzations for student & Hispanic engineers, and a student-run cooperative living organization. I hope that my portfolio helps to bring you an understanding of my passion for web design. Thank you very much!
